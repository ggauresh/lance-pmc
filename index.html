Lance PMC

Lance Project Management Consultancy (PMC) Pty Ltd is a Victorian based Consultancy Company which provides services for managing construction projects as per clients’ requirements.

Lance operates as centre of excellence through a proactive management that takes into account characteristics of the market, requirement of the client, environment, and social responsibilities. Individually, we are encouraged to view each project as an opportunity to exceed “Client’s Expectations” through the provision of the highest quality services.

We have over 6 years’ experience and a track record in delivering major bridge projects, roads and transport infrastructure projects with top tier Contractors.

At Your Service

Pre-Contracts Estimating & Procurement

We offer assistance with quantity and cost estimation, procurement, programming, staging and risk analysis during target outturn cost phase.

Construction Planning

To ensure that your construction project is completed successfully, robust planning and engineering management support is vital. Our services during construction assist you with operational support during procurement, delivery and execution phase to effectively keep up with the planned schedule.
With our extensive experience covering major aspects of construction, we offer you the skills needed to execute infrastructure and building projects – regardless of the of their size.

Construction Project Management

We have major construction project management experience related to bridges, rail infrastructure, tanking structure in tunnels and level crossing removal projects. We could also assist you in commercial and residential sector of construction.
